What is Ethefux?

Ethefux uses inovative Blockchain technology to create a secure solution to the current credit score system. Blockchain is publicly browsable therefore there is no fees to view your full credit history.



Why should I use Ethefux instead of the current system?

The current credit score system is flawed in many ways:

  1. Organisations that are responsible for calculating your credit score often make mistakes.
  2. Organisations that are responsible for the safety of your credentials do not care enough to fix bugs within their software to keep your data safe, for whatever reason.
  3. Human error in calculation is common, removing the human element to the credit system would make it much more stable and trustworthy.
Ethefux removes the need for the old credit score system and replaces it by using Blockchain technology to ensure that user credentials and data are safe. Blockchain does this by making the records of every transaction that has occurred avaliable to everybody using the Blockchain. This means that if there is any malicious activity, or something does not add up, it can be spotted and put right very easily. This makes mass data breaches almost impossible. There is also no such thing as rolling back within a Blockchain, which means that there is no way to change or edit data from previous blocks. This is implemented as all the blocks are linked together in a chain (hence the name Blockchain), and there are unique values for each block that are also present on the block that follows on from it.


Why should I trust Blockchain?

Blockchain technology uses distributed ledgers for transactions. In doing this, the risk of a single point of failure is taken away therefore Blockchain provides end-to-end privacy and encryption while still ensuring convenience for all users.


How is my credit score calculated?

With Ethefux, your credit score is determined by the amount of contracts you have entered into and the consistency of your repayments. You will be able to keep track of your contracts and loan repayments very easily on the 'dashboard', in which you will be able to track your green loans and red loans: loans you have made the payment for this month and those you have not. You will also be able to scan your QR code from a selected loan you still have to pay from your red loans, to pay your loan in full using your smartphone.
